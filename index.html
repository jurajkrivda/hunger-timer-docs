Privacy Policy

Hunger Timer App

Summary: Hunger Timer respects your privacy. All your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Storage

Hunger Timer stores all data locally on your device and optionally syncs via your personal iCloud account:

  • Meal records - timestamps and meal sizes you log
  • App preferences - your settings and customizations
  • Purchase status - whether you have purchased Premium features

This data is stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No personal identifiers
  • No location data
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data sent to external servers

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your meal data may sync across your devices via Apple's iCloud service. This sync is:

  • Controlled by your device settings
  • Encrypted by Apple
  • Accessible only to you

We do not have access to your iCloud data.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles purchase data.

Apple Watch & Widgets

The Apple Watch app and widgets access the same local data as the main app to display your meal timing information. No additional data is collected.

Children's Privacy

Hunger Timer does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is designed for general audiences and contains no age-restricted content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
