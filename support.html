Frequently Asked Questions

How do I record a meal?

Tap the main button on the home screen and select your meal size (small, medium, or large).

What do the colors mean?

Red means it's too soon to eat, yellow means you can eat if hungry, green means it's the optimal time to eat, and blue means you may have waited too long.

How do I sync data between devices?

Your data syncs automatically via iCloud. Make sure iCloud is enabled in your device settings.

What's included in Premium?

Premium unlocks advanced statistics, custom hunger zones, meal notes, daily insights, and streak tracking.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases".
